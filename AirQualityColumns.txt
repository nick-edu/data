Dataset of air quality from 5 chemical sensors located at road level in an Italian city.
Source: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Air+Quality

Columns:
CO: true hourly averaged concentration CO in mg/m^3 (reference analyzer)
TnOtCO: hourly averaged sensor response (nominally CO targeted)
NMHC: true hourly averaged overall Non-Metanic Hydrocarbons concentration in mg/m^3 (reference analyzer)
C6H6: true hourly averaged benzene concentration in mg/m^3 (reference analyzer) 
TiO2tNMHC: titania hourly averaged sensor response (nominally NMHC targeted)
NOx: true hourly averaged NOx concentration in ppb (reference analyzer)
WO3tNOx: tungsten oxide hourly averaged sensor response (nominally NOx targeted)
NO2: true hourly averaged NO2 concentration in mg/m^3 (reference analyzer)
WO3tNO2: tungsten oxide hourly averaged sensor response (nominally NO2 targeted)
In2O3tO3: indium oxide hourly averaged sensor response (nominally O3 targeted)
Temp: temperature in deg. C
RelHum: relative humidity (%)
AbsHum: absolute humidity
DateTime: date and time
Month: month
DayOfWeek: day of the week, 0-based
Year: year
Day: day
Hour: hour
