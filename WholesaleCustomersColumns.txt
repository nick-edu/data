Dataset with spending on various products. All columns except Channel and Region show annual spending on the respective product.
Source: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/wholesale+customers

Columns:
Channel: Horeca (Hotel/Restaurant/Cafe) or Retail
Region: Lisbon, Oporto or Other
Fresh: annual spending on fresh products
Milk: annual spending on milk products
Grocery: annual spending on grocery products
Frozen: annual spending on frozen products
Detergents_Paper: annual spending on detergents and paper products
Deli: annual spending on delicatessen products
